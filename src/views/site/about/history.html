    The first principal

    After the school's independence, Novaliches - San Bartolome Annex finally became San Bartolome High School. Dr. Proceso T. Lera was also the first principal of San Bartolome High School.

  • 2001

    Sixth Head teacher-in-charge

    Dr. Proceso T. Lera assumed his position as the newly appointed Head Teacher In-charge and the first male administrator of the school.

    His determined and relentless effort in pursuing the independence of the school paved the way for its autonomy on March 6, 2002, which was approved by Secretary Raul Roco.
